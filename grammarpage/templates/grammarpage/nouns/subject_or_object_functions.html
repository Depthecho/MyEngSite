{% translate "Subject/Object Functions" %}

{% translate "Nouns play a crucial role in sentences by performing actions or being affected by them. The main functions of a noun in a sentence are to act as the subject or the object." %}

{% translate "The Subject" %}

{% translate "The subject of a sentence is the noun or pronoun that performs the action of the verb. It usually comes before the main verb." %}

{% translate "The Object" %}

{% translate "The object is the noun or pronoun that receives the action of the verb. There are several types of objects." %}
