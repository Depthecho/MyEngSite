{% translate "A collective noun for animals is a specific word used to refer to a group of a particular species. Many of these terms have been used for centuries and add a colorful and traditional flavor to the English language." %}

{% translate "Common Collective Nouns for Animals" %}

{% translate "These are some of the most widely recognized collective nouns for animal groups." %}

{% translate "Collective Noun" %} {% translate "Animal" %} {% translate "Example" %}
herdcattle, elephants, deerA large herd of cattle was grazing in the field.
flockbirds, sheep, goatsA flock of birds flew south for the winter.
packwolves, dogsThe pack of wolves hunted together.
schoolfishA school of fish swam by the coral reef.
swarminsects, beesA huge swarm of bees buzzed around the hive.
