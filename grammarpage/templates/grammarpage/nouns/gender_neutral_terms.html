{% translate "Gender-Neutral Terms" %}

{% translate "A gender-neutral term is a word that does not specify a person's gender. The use of these terms is becoming increasingly important in modern English to promote inclusivity and avoid bias." %}

{% translate "Why Use Gender-Neutral Terms?" %}

{% translate "Using gender-neutral language is a way of showing respect for all people, regardless of their gender identity. It helps to:" %}

  • {% translate "Avoid making assumptions about a person's gender." %}
  • {% translate "Include people who are non-binary or do not identify as male or female." %}
  • {% translate "Promote equal opportunities by not associating certain professions with a specific gender." %}
